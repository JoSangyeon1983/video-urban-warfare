### 공통 권고 / 결정
1. **크기 명시** — 전부 `1536x1024`(가로). gpt-image-1은 1024²/1536×1024/1024×1536만 지원.
2. **과적 해소** — 객체 수 ~10~20 한계. **사용자 결정: 컨셉마다 별도 시트로 전면 분리**(장비·액션 합본도 분리, 한 장 욱여넣기 폐기). Codex 분리 권고 채택. 단일 컨셉 시트 내부는 요소만 축소.
3. **인-이미지 라벨 최소화** — GPT 텍스트 렌더링 불안정 → 짧은 라벨만, 최종 라벨은 Photoshop/Figma 등 후처리.
4. **일관성 워크플로** — ①캐릭터 시트 먼저 생성→베스트 1장을 **앵커 이미지**로 → ②장비 시트 생성 시 앵커를 **참조 이미지로 첨부**. 드론은 별도 생성.
5. **안전 표현(미적용)** — Codex는 드론 무기 표현 완화 권장. **사용자 결정: 완화 되돌림** — 원본("armed combat drone", "machine-gun pods", "weapon hardpoints", "prop carbine")이 ChatGPT에서 문제없이 생성됨.

## 이미지 프롬프트 (컨셉당 1장)
**컨셉마다 별도 시트** — 캐릭터 / 장비(택티컬 백팩) / 액션 포즈 / 무장(카빈) / 히어로 / 적 드론. (※ "여러 컨셉을 한 장에 욱여넣기"는 폐기 — 장비와 액션을 한 장에 합치지 않고 분리함.) 크기 명시, 라벨 최소화(최종 라벨은 후처리), 무기 표현 완화는 미적용.

> **앵커 규칙(중요)**: 참조로 첨부하는 앵커는 **채택(확정)한 결과물 이미지만** 사용함. 아직 생성 안 했거나 **반려한 이미지는 앵커로 쓰지 않음.**
> **생성 순서**: ① 캐릭터 시트 → 베스트 1장 앵커 확정 → ② 택티컬 백팩(FOLDED 정본 확정 → 그 이미지로 DEPLOYED 파생 → 부스터) → ③ 액션 포즈(캐릭터 앵커, 가능하면 백팩 정본도) → ④ 카빈 캐릭터컷(캐릭터 앵커) → ⑤ 히어로(확정된 캐릭터+백팩+카빈 첨부). 적 드론은 별도.

### 캐릭터 시트 (앵커 ✅ 확정)
참조 틀: [[캐릭터 시트 프롬프트 템플릿]].

**생성 결과 — 앵커 확정**
- 생성1 (2026-06-18): ![[캐릭터시트_생성1_20260618.png]]
  - FRONT/SIDE/BACK + HELMET ON/OFF + PALETTE 정상. **이 이미지를 한서진 앵커로 확정** → 이후 장비·액션·카빈·히어로 생성 시 참조 첨부.
```
Output size: 1536x1024 landscape.

2D anime character reference sheet, Korean/Japanese anime style, clean lineart, cel shading, high detail, plain white background, professional model sheet layout. Use a clean 3-column by 2-row layout with generous spacing; prioritize consistent character design over decorative detail.

Create the canonical reference design for LT. HAN SEOJIN, a late-20s Korean female special powered-armor soldier in a near-future military sci-fi setting. Slim athletic build, long legs, calm sharp eyes, composed battle-hardened expression. Short dark brown-black practical hair, visible when the helmet is off.

Armor: slim tactical powered exosuit, sleek and functional, not bulky. Matte navy and gunmetal gray main armor, dark undersuit, small orange accent lights. Semi-transparent visor helmet partially revealing the face. A tactical backpack on the back (a compact jetpack with folded fictional pod units integrated), forearm tactical devices, lower-leg booster units.

Layout:
- Top row: three full-body neutral standing views — FRONT, SIDE, BACK.
- Bottom row: two portrait close-ups — HELMET ON, HELMET OFF — plus four clean color swatches (matte navy, gunmetal gray, orange accent, dark undersuit).

Use only short, widely-spaced English labels (FRONT, SIDE, BACK, HELMET ON, HELMET OFF, PALETTE), separated from the art; no tiny technical annotations. Keep face, proportions, armor silhouette, helmet, tactical backpack, and colors identical across all views.
```

### 장비 시트 — 택티컬 백팩 (앵커: 캐릭터 시트)
> ⚠️ 구 "장비·액션 시트(한 장)"를 **장비 / 액션 포즈로 분리**함(한 장 욱여넣기 폐기).
> ⚠️ **상태 변환을 한 장에 같이 요구하지 않음**(2026-06-19 반려 반영). 한 장에 FOLDED+DEPLOYED+부스터를 함께 뽑았더니 ① 접힘↔펼침이 기계적으로 불가능하게 어긋나고(셀 배열·외곽 불일치) ② 부스터 방향이 전신샷(정강이 뒤)과 디테일(정강이 옆) 불일치함 → ![[택티컬백팩시트_불일치_20260619.png]] (**반려, 앵커로 쓰지 않음**). 원인: 단일 생성은 "같은 물체의 다른 상태"를 추론 못 하고 각 칸을 독립적으로 그림.
> ✅ **해결 — 단계별 앵커 연쇄**: ① FOLDED 정본을 단독 생성·확정 → ② 그 이미지를 **참조로 첨부**해 DEPLOYED를 파생 → ③ 부스터는 별도. 메커니즘은 **클램셸**(닫힘=박스, 펼침=커버만 바깥으로 열림), 셀은 **세기 쉬운 격자**로 축소, 좌우 대칭. 전부 미생성.

**① FOLDED 정본 (1024x1024, 단독 오브젝트)** — 캐릭터 앵커 첨부. 베스트 1장을 "접힘 정본"으로 확정해 ②의 참조로 씀.
```
Output size: 1024x1024.

Use the approved LT. HAN SEOJIN character sheet as the visual reference for the backpack's design, color, and proportions.

2D anime mecha equipment design, Korean/Japanese anime style, clean lineart, cel shading, plain white background. A fictional anime mecha design, not a real-world weapon; avoid realistic engineering detail; prioritize a readable silhouette.

Subject: the TACTICAL BACKPACK as a standalone object (no character), in its FOLDED/closed state. A compact, left-right symmetric clamshell housing centered on a vertical spine, with an integrated compact jetpack. Matte navy and gunmetal gray with small orange accent lines, matching the character.

Show two clean views of the SAME object: a large three-quarter rear view and a smaller side view. Closed clamshell — the side covers are shut, hiding the internal cells. Compact and elegant, not bulky.

Use only short labels (REAR, SIDE). This is the canonical CLOSED form — keep the outline, panel shapes, and proportions clean and memorable.
```

**② DEPLOYED 파생 (1024x1024)** — **①의 FOLDED 정본 이미지를 참조로 첨부**(캐릭터 앵커 아님). 미생성.
```
Output size: 1024x1024.

Use the attached FOLDED tactical-backpack image as the EXACT design reference.

2D anime mecha equipment design, same style, clean lineart, cel shading, plain white background.

Show the SAME unit from the attached image in its DEPLOYED/open state: the two side clamshell covers hinge outward about 90 degrees like wings, revealing an internal grid of stylized capsule cells (a clean, countable grid, e.g. 3 rows x 3 columns per side).

Keep IDENTICAL to the reference: the central spine, overall outline, panel shapes, color, and proportions. Only the covers rotate open and the internal cell grid becomes visible. Same three-quarter rear view as the reference.

Capsule cells read as compact fictional anime capsules, not realistic missiles. Use only short label (DEPLOYED). Readable and uncluttered.
```

**③ 부스터 디테일 (1024x1024, 옵션)** — 캐릭터 앵커 첨부. 캐릭터 시트에 이미 보이므로 선택. 각도·장착위치 고정이 핵심.
```
Output size: 1024x1024.

Use the approved LT. HAN SEOJIN character sheet as the visual reference.

2D anime mecha equipment detail, same style, clean lineart, cel shading, plain white background.

Subject: the lower-leg BOOSTER unit, shown on her lower leg from a three-quarter REAR view matching the character sheet. The booster is mounted on the BACK of the calf (rear of the lower leg), NOT on the outer side. Matte navy and gunmetal gray with small orange accents.

Single clean detail view. Use only short label (BOOSTER). Keep the mount position and orientation consistent with the character sheet.
```

### 액션 포즈 시트 — JET JUMP / BARRAGE (앵커: 캐릭터 시트, 가능하면 장비 시트도)
참조 틀: [[스타일 적용 규칙]](BARRAGE는 [[이타노 서커스]] 키워드 추가 가능).
> 실생성 피드백 반영: JET JUMP 안정 자세(양팔 차렷·양다리 곧게 모음), BARRAGE 트레일은 미사일 뒤쪽에만, POD 디자인은 장비 시트와 일치.
> 참고(반려): 구 장비·액션 합본 생성1 — ![[장비액션시트_생성1_20260618.png]] (JET JUMP 불안정·POD 불일치·트레일 엉킴으로 반려 → 분리로 대체. **앵커로 쓰지 않음.**)
```
Output size: 1536x1024 landscape.

Use the approved LT. HAN SEOJIN character sheet (and, if available, the approved equipment sheet) as visual references. Preserve her face, proportions, armor, palette, helmet, and tactical backpack (jetpack + backpack pod system).

2D anime action pose sheet for one character, Korean/Japanese anime mecha style, clean lineart, cel shading, plain light-gray background. Two large, readable full-body poses:
(1) JET JUMP — a streamlined vertical flight launch: both arms held straight down along the sides, both legs fully extended and held straight together (no raised or bent knee), body aligned and balanced, leg boosters firing downward.
(2) BARRAGE — backpack pods deployed, many small stylized capsules launching outward; each capsule's light/smoke trail appears ONLY behind it, trailing in its wake, never in front of or tangled across the capsules.

Keep the pod system design identical to the equipment sheet (same shape, segment/cell count). Stylized fictional capsules, not realistic missiles. Use only short labels (JET JUMP, BARRAGE). Dynamic but readable; no extra insets.
```

### 적 드론 시트 (별개)
> 한 장에 맞춰 추림. **포함**: 정면/측면/후면 뷰 · 강화복 군인 스케일 실루엣 · 머신건 포드 디테일 1개(5요소). **제외(필요시 교체)**: 탑뷰, 센서 터렛·팬 인셋, 파손 실루엣.
```
Output size: 1536x1024 landscape.

2D anime mechanical enemy drone reference sheet, Korean/Japanese anime military sci-fi style, clean lineart, cel shading, high detail, plain white background.

Subject: a large near-future armed combat drone used in ruined urban warfare, 2-3x larger than a powered-armor soldier. Heavily armored central body, side-mounted ducted fans or compact thruster units, visible machine-gun pods, weapon hardpoints, a lower sensor turret, and rear booster nozzles. Aggressive, functional, readable silhouette. Dark gray and gunmetal body, small red sensor lights, weaponized industrial design.

Keep the sheet to these elements only, clearly spaced in one image:
- Three large orthographic views: FRONT, SIDE, REAR.
- One simple scale-comparison silhouette of a powered-armor soldier beside the FRONT view (drone clearly much larger).
- One detail callout of a machine-gun pod.

Use only short labels (FRONT, SIDE, REAR, SCALE, GUN POD). Make it look like a large armed combat drone that can chase a jetpack soldier between ruined elevated highway pillars; keep it readable from a distance. Do not make it look like a toy drone, insect, bird, jet fighter, spaceship, or cute robot.
```

### 무장(카빈) — 캐릭터 장착 컷 (앵커: 캐릭터 시트 ✅ A안 통과)

### 히어로 키비주얼 (장비 완비)
> 장비 완비 풀바디 히어로 컷. 배경은 스토리 미확정이라 중립/최소로 둠(지어내지 않음).
> ⚠️ **앵커는 확정된 이미지만 첨부**. 현재 확정: **캐릭터 시트 ✅, 카빈 캐릭터컷 ✅**. **장비 시트는 미생성/미확정** → 생성·채택 후 함께 첨부(반려한 구 장비·액션 합본은 쓰지 않음). 캐릭터 시트에 백팩 포드가 보이므로, 장비 시트 확정 전이라도 캐릭터+카빈 앵커만으로 진행 가능.
```
Output size: 1024x1536 portrait.

Use the approved (accepted) LT. HAN SEOJIN character sheet and the approved carbine character cut as visual references; add the approved equipment sheet once it exists. Preserve LT. HAN SEOJIN's exact face, hairstyle, proportions, armor, palette, helmet, tactical backpack (jetpack + backpack pod system), and carbine design. Do not redesign. Style must not override the reference character.

2D anime key visual, Korean/Japanese anime cinematic style, clean lineart, cel shading, dramatic lighting. A polished hero shot of LT. HAN SEOJIN fully equipped: slim tactical powered exosuit, semi-transparent visor helmet, the tactical backpack mounted (a compact jetpack with the backpack pod system), holding her near-future tactical carbine (conventional layout with underbarrel launcher).

Single full-body dynamic hero pose, readable silhouette, confident and battle-ready. Plain or softly suggested neutral background — keep focus on the character, no specific scene.

Fictional stylized equipment only (capsule-style pods, prop carbine), not real-world weapons. Prioritize character accuracy and a clean, striking composition.
```
